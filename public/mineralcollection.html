My Small Obsession

Ever since I was a kid, I had a passion for the Earth around me, and especially the rocks and minerals that make it up. Here you will find a repository of every mineral I own, whether that be through purchases, or rockhounding. I hope you enjoy looking through this digital museum and perhaps learn something!
