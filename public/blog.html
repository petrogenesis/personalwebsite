My Blog

Where adventure meets casual conversation.

Welcome to my personal blog

Growing up on the East end of Long Island, I had plenty of access to the things I loved, such as the Ocean, beautiful sunsets on the bay, fishing, and collecting various rocks from the beach. I've always had a passion for the nature around me, yet, I often struggled to find ways to share these passions with other people. It was always a major goal of mine to find ways to convey just how enjoyable the outdoors can be to others, in an easy and convenient way.

This is where this blog comes in, which I hope will serve as a valuable resource to showcase things I care about. Furthermore, I hope these things serve an educational purpose, or at the very least, are of interest to people who may be seeking guidance or information on the things they too, love. While the topics I present here my vary, I am sure there will be something for everyone to read about!

I encourage readers to not only submit ideas, but contact me if they have any questions, comments, concerns, or topics they would like to discuss regarding my blog. Thank you for taking the time to read this page, and I hope you find something of value or interest here. All the best,

Cameron
